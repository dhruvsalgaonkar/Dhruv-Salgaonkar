What is a Matoli?

Matoli is a traditional festive decoration created during Ganesh Chaturthi in Goa and nearby regions. It consists of various seasonal fruits, vegetables, leaves, roots, flowers, and natural items that are collected from the surroundings and hung around the idol of Lord Ganesha.
